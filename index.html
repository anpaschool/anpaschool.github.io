About Winter School, 2020

Welcome to the ANPA Winter School 2020!

We are greatly excited with the new enthusiasm to introduce our third ANPA school on scientific computing in Python. We delivered out two schools of computing in 2019 Winter, and 2019 Summer. Our past experience with participants has elevated our teaching style and tools.

The rapidly developing cloud computing infrastructure has facilitated parallel and distributed computing in very low cost. The quantity of data accumulated through simulation, lab experiments and other human activities has far exceeded the human capacity of manual curation and analysis, necessitating novel computing tools to extract the insights from large volumes of data. Our attempt in this winter school is to introduce the cutting age scientific computing tools and algorithms in Python.

We have developed a syllabus and projects to make participants familiar with fundamental of computing as well as standard methods in computing, including Python programming ( e.g., introduction to function, class and data-structure), numerical methods (e.g., solving differential equations), simulation (e.g., intoduction to Monte Carlo simulation), optimization( introduction to Machine Learning). Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Bigdata tools and finally Quantum Computing.

ANPA is dedicated to encourage collaboration between Nepali scientist, students and interested all in short and long term research and intern projects.We wish you all the best in your learning path!
